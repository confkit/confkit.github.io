Who are we ?

ConfKit is a French non-profit organization. Our goal is to promote knowledge transfers in the tech industry.

We think the only way to be a better developer is to learn from others - but the best way to become an expert is to teach others!

For now, our main focus is on FrenchKit, the iOS and macOS developer conference in Paris. But we have some cool projects in mind for the future!
